Stir-Fried Rice

Yields: 1

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of fridge cold rice

  • 1 tomato, diced

  • 1/4 onion, diced

  • 1 egg

  • 2 tbsp of soy sauce

  • 1/4 tsp of salt, adjust as needed

  • 1 tsp of pepper, adjust as needed

  • 1/2 tsp of cumin powder, adjust as needed

  • 1/2 tsp of paprika, adjust as needed

  • 1/2 tsp of garlic powder, adjust as needed

  • 1/4 pound of preferred choice of meat and or seafood

  • Some cilantro and green onions for garnishing

  • Oil, as needed

Steps:

  1. In a large pan, put some oil and saut√© the tomato and onion, with some salt
    and pepper, for a few minutes until they become soft and the onions start to
    change color.

  2. Add the meat and or seafood into the pan and fry for about 5 minutes.

  3. In a small bowl, beat the egg and toss it into the pan along with the rice
    and the rest of the seasonings and the soy sauce and fry for 10 minutes or so,
    making sure to break up the rice so there are no clumps. Add more oil
    as necessary to make sure the rice doesn't stick to the pan.

  4. Top with the cilantro and green onion and serve while warm.
