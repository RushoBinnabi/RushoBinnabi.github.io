Chili Oil


Ingredients:

  • All-Purpose Flour: 1 1/2 Cups

  • Salt: 1/2 tsp

  • Sugar: 3/4 Cup

  • Eggs: 2

  • Vanilla Extract: 1 tsp

  • Softened Butter: 1 stick

  • Baking Soda: 1 tsp

  • Ripe Bananas: 3

  • Optional for topping: 1/2 cup of chocolate chips or nuts

Steps:

  1. Put the chili flakes into a large, heat safe bowl or jar and set aside.

  2. Pour the spices and aromatics along with the oil into a sauce over
    medium to low heat and gently and periodically stir until the oil
    reaches about 225°F (110°C).

  3. Strain the infused oil into the chili flakes, stir to combine and leave
    to cool.
