Potato Pierogies

Yields: 1 serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 large potatoes

  • 1/2 cup of bread crumbs

  • 2 eggs

  • A few tablespoons of milk

  • 1/2 tsp of salt

  • 1 tsp of pepper

  • 1/2 garlic powder

  • Oil, as needed

  • Cilantro, for garnishing

Steps:

  1. Fill up a large saucepan or pot with some water, sprinkle with salt, and bring to a boil.

  2. While the water comes to a boil, peel and cut the potatoes into quarters and toss into the
    pot of boiling water.

  3. After the potatoes are fork tender, drain and set aside, for now.

  4. In a small bowl, beat the egg with the milk.

  5. After the potatoes have cooled, using a potato ricer or a masher, mash the potatoes into
    a bowl. Add the seasonings with the potatoes and mix to combine.

  6. Coat the cakes in the egg and the bread crumbs until well coated. Shake off any excess.

  7. In a frying pan, heat some oil to 325-375°F (163-190°C) and fry until golden brown.

  8. Top with the cilantro and serve with your preferred sauce.
