White Bread

Yields: 1

Ingredients:

  • All-Purpose or Bread Flour: 3 1/2 Cups

  • Salt: 1/2 tsp

  • Sugar: 1 tsp

  • Water or Milk: 1 Cup

  • Dry or Instant Yeast: 1 packet or 1 to 1 1/2 tsp

  • Eggs: 1

  • Egg Wash (Optional): 1 Egg and a Splash of Milk

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C).

  2. Lightly grease and flour your preferred size bread pan.

  3. If using dry yeast, put the yeast into a cup filled with 3/4 of the way with
    either water or milk with a pinch of sugar and leave covered on the side for 10-15
    minutes and allow to bloom. After it has bloomed or if using instant yeast, combine
    yeast, flour, salt, eggs, and milk or water in a large bowl until the dough starts to come together.

  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for 10 minutes or until the dough
    starts to form a smooth ball. Adjust with more milk or water as needed.

  5. Put the kneaded dough into a bowl, making sure to brush the dough and the bowl with 1-2 tbsp
    of oil, cover, and leave to proof for an hour.

  6. Transfer to a clean surface and start forming the dough into a log, making sure to close any gaps
    in the dough and transfer into the prepared bread pan. Cover and leave to proof for another hour.

  7. Brush with egg wash if desired and transfer to oven and bake for 30 minutes until the bread sounds
    hollow in the middle.

  8. Brush with butter if desired and leave to cool for about 30 minutes before cutting and serving.


Storage Tips:

  • Do not store in the fridge as it will stale fast.

  • Keep in a cool area and lightly covered.

  • Lasts for days if stored properly.
