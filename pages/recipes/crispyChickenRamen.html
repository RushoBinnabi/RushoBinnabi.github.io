Crispy Chicken Ramen

Yields: 1 Serving




Ingredients: Quantities:
Chicken Breast 1
Tomato 1/2, diced
Onion 1/4, thinly sliced
Mushrooms 2, sliced
Eggs 2
Nissin Hot & Spicy Ramen or any kind of Ramen 1
Salt 1 tsp, adjust as needed
Black Pepper 1/4 tsp, adjust as needed
Cumin Powder 1/4 tsp
Paprika 1/4 tsp
Garlic Powder 1/4 tsp
Bread Crumbs 1/2 cup
Lemon 1/4
Oil as needed

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, place the pack of Ramen along with its flavor packets.

  2. Saut√© together the diced onion, tomato, and sliced mushrooms along
    with some oil and half of the seasonings until the tomatoes and onions are soft
    and changed color, and the mushrooms are cooked through. Put them into the
    bowl with the Ramen and set aside.

  3. In a small bowl, beat one of the eggs with some milk and in another separate bowl,
    combine the bread crumbs with the rest of the seasoning and the juice from the lemon.

  4. Dip the chicken into the egg mixture until the chicken is well coated. Transfer to
    bread crumbs and coat well.

  5. Fry until the chicken reaches 165°F (74°C). While the chicken cooks, fill the bowl
    of Ramen with some boiling water until the Ramen is just about submerged and leave covered.

  6. After the chicken is cooked, rest for 10 minutes. After it has cooled, cut against the grain.

  7. Take the remaining egg mixture and quickly scramble in low heat with the leftover bread crumbs.

  8. Place the chicken and the scrambled eggs into the Ramen, and enjoy.
