Chimichurri




Ingredients: Quantities:
Olive Oil or Mustard Oil 1/4 cup, adjust as needed
Chili Flakes 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Black Pepper 1 tsp
Vinegar 3-4 tbsp
Cilantro 1/2 cup, adjust as needed
Garlic 2-3 cloves
Oregano 1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Mix everything until combined. Adjust as needed.

  2. Use as desired.
