Mashed Potatoes

Yields: 2-3 servings




Ingredients: Quantities:
Potatoes 4-6, peeled or unpeeled
Salt as needed
Black Pepper as needed
Rosemary as needed
Thyme as needed
Garlic Powder as needed
Butter 2 tbsp
Milk 3/4 cups

Steps:

  1. In a pot, combine the milk, a tablespoon of butter, garlic powder,
    rosemary and thyme and bring to a simmer. Set aside.

  2. Cut the potatoes into quarters and bring to a boil in a pot of water.
    Boil until fork tender.

  3. Drain and use a potato ricer to mash the potatoes into the same pot.

  4. Add the liquid to the potatoes and mix to combine. Season with the salt
    and black pepper to taste and add a tablespoon of butter. Continue mixing
    until butter is incorporated into the potatoes.

  5. Serve.
