Buttered Noodles

Yields: 1 serving




Ingredients: Quantities:
Pasta Noodles 1 cup
Softened Butter 1 tbsp
Salt 1 pinch, adjust as needed
Black Pepper 1 pinch, adjust as needed
Choice of Meat (optional) 1/4 pounds, ground
Oil as needed

Steps:

  1. In a large saucepan, boil the pasta noodles in some salted water for 10 minutes
    or until al dente. Strain and put back into the pan with the softened butter and the seasonings.
    Mix until the butter is fully melted into the pasta noodles and everything is combined.

  2. In a separate saucepan, sauté the meat with some of the seasoning until cooked through.

  3. Add the sautéed meat into the saucepan with the noodles and mix until everything is
    incorporated and adjust the seasoning as needed.

  4. Serve and enjoy.
