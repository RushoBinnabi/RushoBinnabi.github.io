BBQ Spice Rub

Yields: 1 serving




Ingredients Quantities
Salt 1 tbsp
Black Pepper 1 tbsp
Paprika 1 tbsp
Garlic Powder 1 tbsp
Onion Powder 1 tbsp
Brown Sugar 1/2 cup
Chili Powder 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix together all the ingredients until combined.

  2. Use as needed.
