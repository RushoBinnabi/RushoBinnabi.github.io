Breakfast Burritos

Yields: ~12 Burritos




Ingredients: Quantities:
Tortillas or Flatbread 12
Tomato 1/2
Red Onion 1/2
Ground Beef or Chicken 1 pound
Spinach 1 cup
Eggs 4
Salt 1 tsp
Black Pepper: 1 tsp
Garlic Powder 1 tsp
Paprika 1 tsp
Oregano 1 tsp
Oil as needed

Steps:

  1. Fry flatbread until golden brown or the tortillas until toasted and warmed
    through. Lay onto a sheet pan to cool.

  2. In a large pan, sauté the ground beef with some oil and 1/4 of the seasonings.

  3. Remove the beef from the pan and put it to a bowl. Then dice the tomato, onion,
    and potato and put them into a bowl and set aside.

  4. Cook the potatoes with 1/4 of the seasonings until cooked through and soft.

  5. Add back the diced onion and tomato with the potatoes and sauté for 5 minutes.

  6. Add back the ground beef into the pan and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.

  7. Create a well in the middle of the pan. Beat the eggs with some salt and pepper
    and pour it into the pan and using a spatula, making sure to draw the eggs towards
    the center, scrambling them with the rest of the seasonings. Cool completely.

  8. Take some of the filling and put them onto the tortillas or flat bread, making
    sure to not overfill. Gently fold them and tuck in the sides and place them
    back onto the sheet pan as you go.

  9. Put them into the freezer for 1 hour to set.

  10. After 1 hour, transfer them into bags and store them in the freezer.

Thawing Tips:

  • Take a damp paper towel and wrap the burrito in it and microwave for 1:30-2 minutes.
