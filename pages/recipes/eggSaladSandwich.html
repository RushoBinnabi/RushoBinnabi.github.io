Egg Salad Sandwich

Yields: 1-2 sandwiches




Ingredients: Quantities:
Eggs 1
Mayonnaise 2 tbsp
Salt 1 pinch, adjust as needed
Black Pepper 1 pinch, adjust as needed
Paprika 1 pinch, adjust as needed
Bread (optionally toasted) 2, sliced
Cucumbers 2 tbsp, diced
Onion 2 tbsp, diced

Steps:

  1. In a large saucepan, boil the eggs in some salted walter for 10 minutes.
    Set aside to cool in some ice water. Peel the eggs into a bowl.

  2. Mash the eggs or scoop out the yolks and chop up the whites until
    slightly chunky. Dice the cucumbers and onions.

  3. Combine everything with the seasonings and the mayonnaise until
    well combined.

  4. Lightly spread some mayonnaise on the bread slices and spread
    some of the filling on one of the slices. Cover with the other slice
    and cut off the ends.

  5. Wrap in plastic wrap to make sure the sandwich doesn't fall apart
    and slice diagonally.

  6. Enjoy.
