Scotch Egg

Yields: ~2 Eggs




Ingredients: Quantities:
Eggs 3
Choice of Meat 1 pound, ground
Paprika 1 tsp
Garlic Powder 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Black Pepper 1 tsp
Bread Crumbs 1 cup
All-Purpose Flour 1/2 cup
Oil as needed

Steps:

  1. Boil 2 of the eggs in some salted water for ~6 minutes. Transfer the eggs to
    a bowl of cold water to stop the cooking process. Peel the eggs and set aside.

  2. Heat some oil until the temperature reaches around 325-375°F (162-190°C).

  3. In a bowl, mix the meat with half of the seasonings until well combined.

  4. In two other bowls, mix the bread crumbs with the rest of the seasonings and
    beat the other egg with some milk.

  5. Flatten down the meat mixture over some plastic wrap. Take the eggs and
    gently wrap them around the meat mixture. Be careful not to break the egg.

  6. Take the eggs and pann√© them otherwise known as coating them in the flour,
    then the eggs, and then the breadcrumbs until fully coated.

  7. Fry the eggs until they are golden brown.

  8. Sprinkle some salt to taste and cut in half if desired and enjoy.
