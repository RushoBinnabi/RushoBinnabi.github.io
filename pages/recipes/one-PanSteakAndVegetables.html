One-Pan Steak and Vegetables

Yields: 1 serving




Ingredients for the Steak and Vegetables: Quantities:
Any cut of steak 1
Salt 2 tsp
Black Pepper: 2 tsp
Garlic Powder: 2 tsp
Oil 4 tbsp
Mushrooms 4
Onions 1/2
Bell Peppers 1
Ingredients for the Sauce: Quantity:
Soy Sauce 1/4 cup
Oyster Sauce 2 tbsp
Garlic Clove 1, crushed
Black Pepper 1/2 tsp
Brown Sugar 1 tbsp
Ketchup 2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Rub half of the oil and seasonings on both sides of the steak and set aside.
    In a small bowl, combine everything for the sauce and set aside.

  2. In a hot pan, cook the steak to 135°F (54°C) with a good sear on both sides.
    Wrap in foil and rest for 10 minutes.

  3. While the steak rests, saut√© the vegetables with the rest of the oil and the
    seasonings until slightly charred. Set aside.

  4. Bring the sauce to a simmer in the pan until thickened slightly. Turn off
    the heat and set aside.

  5. Spread some of the sauce on the plate. Cut the steak against the grain and
    place beside the charred vegetables. Drizzle more of the sauce on top of both
    the steak and vegetables.

  6. Enjoy.
