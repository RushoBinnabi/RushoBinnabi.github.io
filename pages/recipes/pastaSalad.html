Pasta Salad

Yields: ~12+ Servings




Ingredients for the Pasta Salad: Quantities:
Choice of Pasta 3-4 cups
Tomato 1, diced
Onion 1/2, sliced
Olives 2 cups, halved
Choice of Peppers 4, sliced
Cheese A handful
Cilantro A handful
Salt to taste
Black Pepper to taste
Ingredients for the Vinaigrette: Quantities:
Olive Oil 1/4 cup
Turmeric 1/2 tsp
Salt to taste
Black Pepper to taste
Garlic Cloves 3, crushed
Vinegar 3-4 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta in some heavily salted water for 8-10 minutes. Strain and set to cool completely.

  2. Slice the olives and dice the other vegetables and put each of them in a separate bowl and set aside.

  3. Mix together the vinaigrette and set aside.

  4. Toss the vegetables with the pasta and the vinaigrette until well combined.

  5. Cover with plastic wrap and chill it in the refrigerator for 2-3 hours.

  6. Serve.
