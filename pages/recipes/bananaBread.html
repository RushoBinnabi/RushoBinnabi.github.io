Banana Bread

Yields: 1 Loaf




Dry Ingredients: Quantities:
All-Purpose Flour 1 1/2 cups
Salt 1/2 tsp
Sugar 3/4 cup
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Topping (optional) Feel free to add your own
Wet Ingredients Quantities:
Softened Butter 1 stick
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Eggs 2
Ripe Bananas 2

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C).

  2. Butter an 8 x 3 inch (20 x 8 cm) bread pan.

  3. Mash the bananas with a fork in a bowl.

  4. In a small bowl, mix together the flour and baking soda and set aside.

  5. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, salt, and sugar
    until well combined. Add in the eggs, mashed bananas and using a plastic spatula,
    vanilla extract, and mix to combine. Add the flour and baking soda mixture and
    continue mixing to combine. Stir in the topping.

  6. Transfer the prepared batter to the prepared bread pan and bake for
    50 to 60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.

  7. Leave to cool for 20 to 30 minutes before cutting and serving.

Storage Tips:

  • Wrap in plastic wrap and keep at room temperature or in a fridge.

  • Stays fresh for a few days if wrapped and kept at room temperature or
    up to a full week if wrapped and kept in the fridge.
