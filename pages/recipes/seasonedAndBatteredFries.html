Seasoned and Battered Fries

Yields: 1 serving




Ingredients: Quantities:
Potatoes 2, skin on
All-Purpose Flour 1 cup
Water enough to make a thin batter
BBQ Spice Rub a few pinches
Oil as needed

Steps:

  1. Heat enough oil to 350-375°F (176-190°C).

  2. In a bowl, mix together the flour and the spice rub.
    Mix with enough water to make a thin batter.

  3. Coat the fries in the batter and fry until golden brown.

  4. Drain on a paper towel and season with the spice rub.

  5. Serve.
