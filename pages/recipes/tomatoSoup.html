Tomato Soup

Yields: 2 servings




Ingredients: Quantities:
Tomatoes 4-5
Garlic Cloves 6-7
Onions 1 1/2
Thyme 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Black Pepper 1 tsp
Oregano 1 1/2 tsp
Chili Flakes (optional) 1/2 tsp
Oil as needed
Water 1/3 cup
Cream 1/4 cup

Steps:

  1. Dice the onions, chop the garlic and sauté in a pan with some oil
    until the onions have softened.

  2. Slice the tomatoes into quarters and sauté with the onions, garlic,
    salt and black pepper for about 5 minutes or until the tomatoes
    have blistered.

  3. Using a blender or an immersion blender, blend together the soup
    with the water until combined.

  4. Pour the mixture back into the pan, mix in the oregano, thyme,
    chili flakes and cream. Simmer for about 5 minutes.

  5. Serve with a drizzle of oil.
