Spicy Stir-Fried Ramen

Yields: 1 serving



Ingredients: Quantities:
Chicken Breast 1, sliced into cubes
Tomato 1/2, diced
Onion 1/3, thinly sliced
Corn 1/4 cup
Egg 1
Samyang Buldak 2x Spicy Chicken Flavor Ramen Stir-Fried Korean Hot Noodle or any kind of Ramen 1
Mushrooms 2, sliced to desired thickness
Bell Peppers 2, sliced to desired size and shape
Oil as needed
Salt as needed
Black Pepper as needed
Garlic Powder as needed

Steps:

  1. In a large saucepan or a stockpot, boil the ramen for about 6-8 minutes and then strain and set aside.
    Reserve some of the starchy water for later.

  2. In a large pan, saut√© the onions, peppers, corn and tomatoes until they have softened. Sprinkle in
    some of the seasoning and stir to combine.

  3. Add in the mushrooms with a pinch of salt and stir until the mushrooms are cooked through.

  4. In a small bowl, beat the egg with some salt and stir it into the pan with the rest of the ingredients.

  5. Stir in the sauce and flake packets from the noodle pack along with the reserved water from earlier.
    Simmer for 2-3 minutes.

  6. Add back in the noodles and stir, making sure everything is incorporated with the sauce.

  7. Enjoy.
