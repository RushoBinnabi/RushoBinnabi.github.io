Steak and Roasted Vegetables with Mashed Potatoes

Yields: 1 serving




Ingredients Quantities:
Steak 1
Salt 1 tsp, adjust as needed
Black Pepper 1 tsp, adjust as needed
Garlic Powder 1 tsp, adjust as needed
Oil as needed
Mashed Potatoes 1 serving
Chimichurri as needed
Onions 1/2, sliced
Bell Peppers 1/2, sliced

Steps:

  1. Rub the steak with some oil, the salt, black peppers and garlic powder
    and set aside to marinate.

  2. On a hot pan, cook the steak until medium-rare. Wrap in foil and leave to
    rest for ~10 minutes.

  3. Saut√© the bell peppers and onions until softened with some char. Set aside.

  4. Lay the bell peppers and onions on one side of the plate and the mashed potatoes
    on the other.

  5. Unwrap and cut the steak against the grain.

  6. Lay the steak onto the mashed potatoes, Spoon on any leftover juices from the
    steak onto it for added flavor and then spoon on the chimichurri.

  7. Enjoy.
