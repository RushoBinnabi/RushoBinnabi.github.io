Sugar Cookies

Yields: 23 cookies (46 if using both halves of the dough)




Ingredients for the Cookies: Quantities:
Butter 3 sticks (24 tbsp)
All-Purpose Flour 4 1/2 cups (more for shaping and dusting the dough)
Sugar 1 1/2 cups
Vanilla Extract 1 1/2 tsp
Eggs 1 whole, 2 yolks
Baking Powder 1/2 tsp
Cream of Tartar (or Lemon Juice as a substitute) 2 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Ingredients for the Glaze: Quantities:
Powdered Sugar 2 cups (more in small amounts if the glaze is too thin)
Salt 1/2 tsp
Water as needed
Egg Whites 1/4 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Choice of Food Colorings a few drops
