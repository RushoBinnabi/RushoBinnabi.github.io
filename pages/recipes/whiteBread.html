White Bread

Yields: 1 Loaf




Ingredients: Quantities:
All-Purpose or Bread Flour 3 1/2 cups
Sugar 1 tsp
Water or Milk 1 1/2 cups
Dry or Instant Yeast 1 packet or 1 to 1 1/2 tsp
Eggs 1 whole and 1 yolk
Egg Wash (optional) 1 beaten egg and some milk

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C).

  2. Lightly grease your preferred size bread pan.

  3. Combine the yeast, flour, eggs, and milk or water in a large bowl until the dough starts to
    come together.

  4. Brush the dough and the bowl with 1-2 tbsp of oil, cover, and leave to proof for an hour or two.

  5. Transfer to a clean surface and start forming the dough into a log, making sure to close any gaps
    in the dough and transfer into the prepared bread pan. Cover and leave to proof for another hour.

  6. Brush with egg wash if desired and transfer to oven and bake for 50 minutes until the bread sounds
    hollow in the middle.

  7. Brush with butter if desired and leave to cool for about 30 minutes before cutting and serving.

Storage Tips:

  • Keep in a cool area and lightly covered.

  • Lasts for days if stored properly.
