Stir-Fried Rice

Yields: 1 Serving




Ingredients: Quantities:
Fridge Cold Rice 2 cups
Tomato 1, diced
Onion 1/4, diced
Egg 1
Soy Sauce 2 tbsp
Salt 1/4 tsp, adjust as needed
Black Pepper 1/4 tsp, adjust as needed
Cumin Powder 1/2 tsp, adjust as needed
Paprika 1/4 tsp, adjust as needed
Garlic Powder 1/2 tsp, adjust as needed
Choice of Meat and or Seafood 1/4 pound
Cilantro and Green Onion a pinch
Oil as needed

Steps:

  1. In a large pan, put some oil and saut√© the tomato and onion, with some salt
    and pepper, for a few minutes until they become soft and the onions start to
    change color.

  2. Add the meat and or seafood into the pan and fry for about 53 minutes.

  3. In a small bowl, beat the egg and toss it into the pan along with the rice
    and the rest of the seasonings and the soy sauce and fry for 5 minutes or so,
    making sure to break up the rice so there are no clumps. Add more oil as
    necessary to make sure the rice doesn't stick to the pan.

  4. Top with the cilantro and green onion and serve while warm.
