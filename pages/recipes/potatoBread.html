Potato Bread

Yields: 1 loaf




Ingredients: Quantities:
All-Purpose or Bread Flour 3 cups
Sugar 3/4 cup
Salt 1 tsp
Potatoes 2-3 large, mashed
Dry or Instant Yeast 1 tsp or 1 packet
Water or Milk 1 1/2 cups
Eggs 1 whole and 1 yolk
Butter 6 tbsp, softened
Eggwash (optional) 1 egg with a splash of milk

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients and mix until a smooth, sticky ball
    of dough forms.

  2. Brush some oil on the dough ball and bowl. Cover and leave to proof for 1-2 hours.

  3. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C) and lightly grease your preferred bread loaf pan
    with some oil or cooking spray.

  4. Wet your hands and start folding the dough into itself, slowly forming a ball.
    Transfer into the pan. Gently cover with plastic wrap and leave to proof for 30
    minutes to an hour.

  5. Brush the top of the loaf with the eggwash, if desired. Bake for 50 minutes.

  6. Brush the top of the loaf with butter and leave to cool completely before cutting
    and serving.

Storage Tips:

  • Keep in a cool area and lightly covered.

  • Lasts for days if stored properly.
