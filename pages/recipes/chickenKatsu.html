Chicken Katsu

Yields: 1 serving




Ingredients for the Chicken: Quantities:
Chicken Breast 1
Rice 1 serving
Salt 1 tsp
Black Pepper 1 tsp
Garlic Powder 1 tsp
Egg 1
All-Purpose Flour 1/2 cup
Bread Crumbs 1/4 cup
Ingredients for the Sauce: Quantities:
Soy Sauce 1/2 cup
Garlic Clove 1, crushed
Black Pepper 1/2 tsp
Brown Sugar 2 tbsp
Lemon Juice (optional) 1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken breast in half. In a small bowl, combine everything
    for the sauce and set aside.

  2. Into 3 separate bowls, combine half of the seasonings with
    the bread crumbs and the flour. Beat the egg with some milk
    in the 3rd bowl. Set aside.

  3. Heat some oil in a pan and coat the chicken breasts in the flour,
    egg, and bread crumbs, shaking off the excess coating.

  4. Fry the chicken breasts until 165°F (74°C). Rest for about 10 minutes.

  5. While the chicken rests, in a small saucepan, bring the sauce to
    a simmer and reduce by a quarter or until thickened slightly. Leave
    to cool.

  6. Cut the chicken against the grain and layer on top of the rice with
    the sauce on top. Garnish with scallions or green onions and sesame
    seeds.

  7. Enjoy.
