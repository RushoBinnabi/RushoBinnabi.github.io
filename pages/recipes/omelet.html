Omelet

Yields: 1 Serving




Ingredients: Quantities:
Eggs 2-3
Small Potato 1, diced
Small Tomato 1, diced
Small Onion 1, diced
Mushrooms 2, diced
Salt 1/2 tsp
Black Pepper 1/2 tsp
Paprika 1/4 tsp
Garlic Powder 1/4 tsp
Cumin Powder 1/4 tsp
Cheese (optional) A small handful
Small chunks of meat (optional) A small handful
Oil as needed


Steps:

  1. Into a medium-ish bowl, crack the eggs.

  2. Add the tomatoes, onion, potato, mushroom along
    with the seasonings, cheese and meat, if desired. Mix
    until combined.

  3. Heat pan with some oil and toss in the combined egg mixture.

  4. Cook on a low heat until the edges have set and the
    potatoes have become soft and are cooked through.
    Serve while warm.
