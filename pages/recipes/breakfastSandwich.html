Breakfast Sandwich

Yields: 1 sandwich




Ingredients: Quantities:
Your preferred bread 2 slices, toasted
Tomato 1 or 2 slices
Mushroom 1, sliced
Onion 1 or 2 thin slices
Avocado 1/2, sliced
Salt A pinch
Black Pepper A pinch
Cumin Powder (optional) A pinch
Egg 1, fried

Steps:

  1. Top the bottom half of the sandwich with the fried egg.

  2. Put the sliced avocado on top of the egg, making sure to
    put the pinch of salt, pepper, and cumin on top of the avocado.

  3. Put the sliced mushrooms on top of the avocado followed by slices
    of tomato and onion on top of the mushrooms and finish with the top
    half of the sandwich.
