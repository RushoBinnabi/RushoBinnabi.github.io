Omelet

Yields: 1

Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 eggs.

  • 1 small potato, diced

  • 1 small tomato, diced

  • 1 small onion, diced

  • 2 mushrooms, diced

  • 1/2 tsp of Salt

  • 1/2 tsp of Pepper

  • 1/4 tsp of Paprika

  • 1/4 tsp of Garlic Powder

  • 1/4 tsp of Cumin Powder

  • Cheese (Optional)

  • Small chunks of meat (Optional)

  • Oil, as needed

Steps:

  1. Into a medium-ish bowl, crack the eggs.

  2. Add the tomatoes, onion, potato, mushroom along
    with the seasonings, cheese and meat, if desired.
    Mix until combined.

  3. Heat pan with some oil and toss in the combined egg mixture.

  4. Cook on a low heat until the edges have set and the
    potatoes have become soft and are cooked through.

  5. Serve while warm.
